Akirakurosawa's treatment of King Lear, with three sons dealing with the passing of an era as their warlord father Lord Hidetoraichimonji , tries to find peace in his last years by dividing his fief among them. While one honest son, Saburonaotoraichimonji , faces his family's wrath for speaking the truth, his two brothers, Tarotakatoraichimonji and Jiromasatoraichimonji  vie for power in the vacuum left by Lordhidetoraichimonji's retirement. While Kyoami the Court Fool  makes wise commentary, Lordhidetoraichimonji discovers for himself the madness caused by his thirst for power, and tries to find redemption for his deadly past.
