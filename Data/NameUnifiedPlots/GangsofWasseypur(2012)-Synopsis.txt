Towards the end of colonial India, Shahidkhan impersonates the legendary Sultanadaku in order to rob British trains. When Sultana discovers his actions, he exiles him from the village. As a result, Shahidkhan becomes a henchman for coalmine owner Ramadhirsingh.ingh. Shahidkhan has ambitions to takeover the coalmine, which angers Ramadhirsingh. Shahidkhan is consequently killed.  Shahidkhan's son, Sardar, is deeply angered and seeks vengeance for the death of his father at the hands of Ramadhirsingh. He makes it his mission to avenge his father.  Ramadhirsingh.ingh becomes a powerful politician. Sardar becomes a fearless gang lord.
